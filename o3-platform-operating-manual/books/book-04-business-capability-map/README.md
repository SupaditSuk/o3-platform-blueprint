# Book 04: Business Capability Map

## Purpose

This book maps the business capabilities of O³ ZONE, describing what the platform does from a business perspective, independent of technology implementation.

## Chapters

1. Workforce Data Management
2. Workforce Analytics and Insights
3. AI-Powered Advisory
4. Survey and Assessment Management
5. Learning and Development (Academy)
6. Subscription and Monetization
7. Customer Relationship Management
8. Benchmark and Market Intelligence
9. Platform Administration
10. Integration and Ecosystem

## Key Takeaways

- Business capabilities describe WHAT the platform does, not HOW
- Each capability maps to one or more products
- Capabilities are stable; implementations evolve
- Capability map guides product roadmap and investment decisions
