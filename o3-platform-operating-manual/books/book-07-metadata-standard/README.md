# Book 07: Insight Engine Architecture

## Purpose

This book defines how the O³ Platform transforms standardized workforce data (OWDS) into business insights, recommendations, actions, and outcomes. The Insight Engine is the analytical brain of O³—the single source of truth for every insight shown in Dashboard, AI Studio, Benchmark Center, and all future products.

## Key Takeaways

- O³ is not a BI tool; it interprets workforce data into business meaning
- Insight Engine is the single source of truth for all insights
- Every insight must include evidence, severity, confidence, recommendation, and action
- Insights are generated from OWDS through the Semantic Layer, KPI Engine, Rule Engine, AI Gateway, and Recommendation Engine
- Dashboard, AI Studio, Benchmark Center, and future products must consume insights from the Insight Engine
- No product should generate its own independent insights
