# Book 02: Business Architecture

## Purpose

This book defines the business architecture of O³ ZONE, including target customers, value proposition, revenue model, subscription packages, freemium strategy, Academy role, CRM strategy, and sales motion.

## Chapters

1. Target Customers and Market Segments
2. Value Proposition and Customer Problems
3. Revenue Model and Subscription Packages
4. Freemium Strategy
5. Academy as Lead Generation Engine
6. CRM Strategy and Customer Journey
7. Sales Motion and Go-to-Market

## Key Takeaways

- Primary target: Thai SMEs 50-500 employees in Manufacturing, Trading, Service
- Five subscription tiers: Basic (Free), Starter, Professional, Business, Enterprise
- Multiple revenue streams: subscription, AI credits, Academy, services, future benchmark
- Academy is primarily a lead generation and education engine
- CRM tracks full customer journey from lead to expansion
