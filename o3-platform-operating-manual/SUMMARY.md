### Key Architecture Decisions

- OWDS as the common workforce data standard
- API-first architecture for all product interactions
- AI Gateway for centralized AI governance
- Insight-first dashboard philosophy (not BI)
- One Simple Company Data Template for MVP onboarding
- Benchmark Center deferred to future phase
- Survey Studio deferred to post-MVP phase

### Target Audience

- Frontend and backend developers
- Product managers and product designers

- Customer success and implementation teams
