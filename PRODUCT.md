# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Thai-first learners, founders, makers, and professionals who want practical writing, online courses, and advisory from Por. Some international visitors need a complete English path from the first launch.

## Product Purpose

The site establishes Por's online presence, grows an audience through writing, and prepares the path toward paid on-demand courses and consulting. Phase 1 succeeds when visitors understand Por's point of view, read or save an article, and have a clear way to follow or contact Por.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, grounded, alive. The brand should feel like a focused knowledge studio: confident enough to lead, quiet enough to read, and fresh enough to remember.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic course landing pages, purple AI gradients, beige creator blogs, glassy SaaS cards, hero metric templates, and agency copy that makes the site feel like Axion Studio instead of Por.

## Design Principles

- Lead with thinking, not decoration.
- Make Thai and English feel equally intentional.
- Use motion as a signal, never as a distraction.
- Keep the first launch lean while leaving a clean path to e-learning.
- Let crimson/coral accents become memorable without overwhelming long-form reading.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible focus states, keyboard-operable navigation and menus, reduced-motion alternatives, readable Thai typography, and no reliance on color alone for meaning.
