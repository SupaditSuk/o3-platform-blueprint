# 04 Product Portfolio

## Product 1: O³ Dashboard

O³ Dashboard is not a traditional BI tool. It is an insight dashboard.

Its purpose is to help executives and HR leaders understand workforce conditions, risks, and priorities.

MVP dashboard areas:

- Workforce Snapshot
- Department Mix
- Age and Tenure Profile
- Turnover
- Regrettable Loss
- Cost of Attrition
- Productivity
- Performance and Talent
- Training
- Employee Sentiment
- AI Summary
- Action Plan

Key principle:

> Every dashboard widget must have an explanation and recommended action.

## Product 2: O³ Workforce AI Studio

O³ Workforce AI Studio is a suite of AI tools that helps HR and business teams do practical work.

Recommended AI tools:

- AI JD Generator
- AI CV Screening
- AI Job Evaluation
- AI Salary Structure Designer
- AI Workforce Planning
- AI Performance Writer
- AI Succession Planning
- AI Merit and Bonus Advisor
- AI Career Path Designer

The product should not feel like generic prompting. It should use guided forms, company context, and structured outputs.

## Product 3: O³ Survey Studio

Survey Studio should be built after the first dashboard and AI MVP.

Scope:

- Employee Experience Survey
- Engagement Survey
- AI Readiness Survey
- Organization Readiness Survey
- 7S Organization Assessment
- Pulse Survey
- Exit Survey

Survey Studio should include:

- Question bank
- Survey distribution
- Response collection
- Score calculation
- Correlation analysis
- Dashboard
- AI summary
- Action planning

Survey Studio is a full product by itself and should not be rushed into MVP unless scope is reduced.

## Product 4: O³ Academy

Academy is a learning and lead generation product.

Initial courses:

- Data-Driven HR with AI
- AI for Executives
- ChatGPT for HR
- AI Business Enablement Framework
- Workforce Intelligence Fundamentals

Academy should connect to products:

- After learning about HR analytics, user is invited to use Dashboard.
- After learning about AI for HR, user is invited to use Workforce AI Studio.
- After learning about employee experience, user is invited to use Survey Studio.

## Future Product: O³ Benchmark Center

Benchmark Center should be future-phase, not MVP.

It becomes valuable only after enough companies use standardized templates.

Benchmark categories:

- Turnover by industry
- Regrettable loss
- Headcount mix
- Training hours
- Revenue per head
- Productivity per head
- Engagement score
- Salary range
- Organization readiness

Benchmark must be anonymous and aggregated.

## Product Relationship

The products should not feel separate. They should follow this logic:

1. **Assess** — understand organization through data and assessment.
2. **Advise** — provide AI insight and recommended action.
3. **Accelerate** — develop capability through tools, templates, and Academy.
