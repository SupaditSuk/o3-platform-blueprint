# 13 Vocabulary and Naming

## Naming Principle

O³ should use clear, business-friendly language. Technical terms can exist internally, but customer-facing language should be simple.

## Product Names

Use:

- O³ Dashboard
- O³ Workforce AI Studio
- O³ Survey Studio
- O³ Academy
- O³ Benchmark Center

Avoid confusing customers with too many sub-brand names in MVP.

## Core Language

Use **Workforce Intelligence** as the main category.

Use **Insight** more than **Report**.

Use **Action Plan** more than **Recommendation** when user needs practical next steps.

Use **AI Advisor** for conversational insight AI.

Use **AI Tools** or **Workforce AI Studio** for task-based tools.

## HR Terms

Recommended terms:

- Employee, not staff, as standard data term.
- Workforce, when referring to the whole people system.
- Headcount, for employee count.
- Turnover, for general employee leaving rate.
- Exit, for individual employee departure record.
- Regrettable Loss, for valuable employee exits.
- Employee Experience, for EX survey and journey.
- Engagement, for psychological connection and motivation.
- Productivity, for output or revenue per head.

## Thai-Friendly Language

For Thai users, avoid overly technical English when possible.

Examples:

- Use “อินไซต์” or “Insight” depending on tone, but explain meaning.
- Replace “Early Warning” with “สัญญาณเตือนล่วงหน้า” for general users.
- Replace “Correlation” with “ความสัมพันธ์ของข้อมูล” where helpful.
- Replace “Dashboard” with “แดชบอร์ด” only if context is Thai-first.

## Package Names

Use:

- Basic
- Starter
- Professional
- Business
- Enterprise

Avoid mixing Free/Growth naming if current official package naming is Basic/Starter/Professional/Business/Enterprise.

## Internal Technical Vocabulary

- OWDS = O³ Workforce Data Standard
- ODKB = O³ Decision Knowledge Base
- AI Gateway = centralized AI routing and governance layer
- Semantic Layer = KPI and business meaning layer
- Event Model = timeline of business and workforce events
- Domain Model = business objects and relationships
